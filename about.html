Our History

Nurturing Excellence Since Inception

Bright Public School (BPS) is a modern educational institution located in Ghantaghar-9, Birgunj, dedicated to providing quality education in a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment. Guided by the motto "Ignite. Innovate. Inspire.", the school focuses on academic excellence, character development, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Through experienced educators, engaging classroom experiences, co-curricular activities, sports, and student-centered learning methods, the institution aims to create responsible citizens who can contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Inspiring Future Innovation

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures innovative, responsible, and confident learners prepared to excel in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

Empowering Holistic Growth

  • Provide quality education through modern teaching practices.
  • Foster creativity, innovation, and critical thinking.
  • Promote leadership, discipline, and ethical values.
  • Create a supportive environment for holistic student development.
Leadership message

Message from the Principal

Welcome to Bright Public School.

At Bright Public School, we are committed to creating an environment where every student can discover their strengths, develop confidence, and achieve academic excellence. Education is not only about acquiring knowledge but also about building character, leadership, creativity, and a sense of responsibility.

Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to provide engaging learning experiences that prepare students for future challenges and opportunities. We encourage curiosity, innovation, and critical thinking while maintaining strong values and discipline. Together with parents and the wider community, we strive to shape future leaders who will contribute positively to society.
