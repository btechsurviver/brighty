Welcome to BPS

Where Learning Meets Excellence

Bright Public School (BPS) is a premier educational institution situated near the C.D.O. Office in Ghantaghar-9, Birgunj. Established with a vision to deliver premium and accessible education, we bridge traditional academic rigor with modern innovations in technology, laboratory experiments, and co-curricular programs.

Our learning philosophy focuses on individual talent, encouraging curiosity, problem-solving abilities, and building confident leaders prepared to address future global opportunities.
